## Romans 1:1–7 — Paul's Mission & Our Calling

The Apostle Paul introduces the gospel and his calling. This lesson explores how Paul's purpose informs our vocation today.

**Key Idea:** Understanding vocation through Paul's calling.

### Reflection Questions

1. What does calling mean for your work?
2. How does the gospel reshape your daily decisions?
